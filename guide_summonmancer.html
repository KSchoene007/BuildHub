Build Overview
✓ Strengths
✓Up to 44 minions simultaneously (with Revives)
✓Extremely safe — minions absorb all damage
✓Best beginner build in D2R — no gear required
✓Ideal for Hardcore mode — nearly unkillable
✓Corpse Explosion clears entire screens instantly
✓Scales from zero budget to endgame gear
✓Trivializes all Terror Zones and Uber content
✗ Weaknesses
✗Slow vs. Physical Immune enemies (need Mages)
✗Enigma required for max efficiency (expensive)
✗Army management — skeletons can get scattered
✗Cold damage from merc shatters corpses (no CE)
✗Rebuilding army after death takes time

Core Mechanic: Raise Skeleton builds your primary army (max 16). Amplify Damage curse makes all physical-immune monsters vulnerable and doubles minion damage. Corpse Explosion is your AoE nuke — it deals 70–120% of a corpse's max life as physical/fire damage in a large radius, clearing entire screens with one click. Decrepify slows and weakens bosses. The army does the work — you manage curses and position.

📊
Stat Allocation
ℹ Stat Rule: The Summonmancer has the simplest stat allocation in D2R. Strength just enough to wear your gear, Dexterity ignored, Energy ignored, everything else into Vitality. Your minions do the damage — you just need to stay alive.
Strength
~60–100
Just enough to equip your gear. Harlequin Crest needs 50, Enigma needs ~65 in Mage Plate base. Never invest more than needed.
Dexterity
Base (25)
Never invest points here. The Summonmancer does not need attack rating, block chance or any dex-based stat. Leave at base 25.
Vitality
Everything
All remaining stat points go here. Target: 1200–1500 Life in endgame. Life is your only real defensive stat — maximize it.
Energy
Base (25)
Never invest points here. Mana is managed through gear (+mana on gear, Insight Runeword on merc provides Meditation aura). Base energy is sufficient.

Skill Tree · Point Distribution
📋 Total Points at Level 99: 98 skill points (Level 2–99) + 12 from quests (4 per difficulty) = 110 total. The distribution below uses 99 points — the remaining 11 are flex points. Color code: Gold = Max (20pts) · Teal = Synergy · Red = 1 point wonder

⚔ SUMMONING SKILLS

Raise Skeleton
20
Primary army builder. Max this first — every point increases skeleton count, damage and life. At 20 points (with +skills gear): up to 16 skeletons simultaneously.
Skeleton Mastery
20
Passive synergy for all skeletons and golems. Max alongside Raise Skeleton — doubles their effective life and damage output. Essential.
Raise Skeletal Mage
20
Mages deal elemental damage — critical for Physical Immune enemies. Max in endgame for variety in damage types (fire, cold, lightning, poison mages).
Clay Golem
1
1-point wonder. Clay Golem slows enemies by 25% on hit — invaluable for boss fights. Used as a meat shield and slow tank, especially for Act bosses.
Golem Mastery
1
1-point wonder. Increases Golem life and speed slightly. One point is sufficient — the Golem is primarily used for its slow and meat-shield value, not damage.
Summon Resist
1
1-point wonder. Grants minions resistances — essential in Nightmare and Hell difficulty where elemental damage can destroy your army. Always take this.
Revive
1
1-point wonder. Raises a monster as a Revive — copies the monster's abilities and stats. Incredibly powerful with the right monsters (e.g. Might aura Wendigos). Up to 18 Revives with +skills gear.

☠ CURSE SKILLS

Amplify Damage
1
MOST IMPORTANT CURSE. 1-point wonder. Reduces physical resistance by 100% — brings Physical Immune monsters to 0 resistance. Doubles all minion damage. Cast this first on any enemy group, always.
Decrepify
1
1-point wonder. Slows enemies by 50%, reduces damage by 50%, reduces physical resistance by 50%. Use on bosses (Diablo, Baal, Mephisto) and Uber Tristram. Switches from Amplify Damage for boss fights.
Lower Resist
1
1-point wonder. Reduces all elemental resistances. Use when fighting elemental immune enemies that your Mages need to hit. Situational but useful in specific farming zones.
Dim Vision
1
1-point wonder. Blinds enemies — they cannot attack at range. Useful in specific dangerous zones (Chaos Sanctuary Oblivion Knights, Durance of Hate) to prevent dangerous spells.

💥 CORPSE SKILLS

Corpse Explosion
20
Your screen-clearing nuke. Deals 70–120% of target corpse's max life as physical/fire damage in a large radius. Max this — it is the primary reason the Summonmancer clears so fast. One click can clear a full screen.
Bone Armor
1
1-point wonder. Absorbs a small amount of physical damage. Situational defensive skill — useful when repositioning through dangerous areas or in Hardcore mode for an extra safety layer.

Playstyle & Rotation

📋 SESSION START (Before Every Game)

1
Switch to Call to Arms (W key)
Cast Battle Command (+1 All Skills) → Cast Battle Orders (doubles Life + Mana). Switch back to Beast. Lasts until you leave the game.
2
Build Your Army
Find corpses or kill the first few enemies manually. Raise Skeleton from every corpse. Then raise Skeletal Mages. Clay Golem last. Army is online in 2–3 minutes of play.
3
Revive Strong Monsters
When you pass Might-aura Wendigos, Act Boss minions, or strong unique monsters — Revive them for extra utility. Up to 18 Revives with +skills gear.

🗺 NORMAL ENEMY GROUPS

1
Amplify Damage
Cast on the largest group of enemies. Reduces physical resistance by 100% — doubles all minion damage instantly. Always cast first.
2
Teleport (Enigma)
Teleport into or near the enemy group. Your skeleton army follows and immediately attacks. One teleport = engage.
3
Corpse Explosion
The moment the first enemy dies → spam Corpse Explosion on the corpse. Chain explosions wipe the entire screen. This is how full maps clear in seconds.
4
Replace Fallen Skeletons
If a skeleton dies — Raise Skeleton from the fresh corpses. Your army self-replenishes from the enemies it kills.

👑 BOSSES (Diablo, Baal, Mephisto)

1
Switch Curse: Decrepify
On bosses use Decrepify instead of Amplify Damage. Decrepify: slows boss 50%, reduces damage 50%, reduces physical resist 50%. Much safer than Amplify Damage vs. bosses.
2
Clay Golem
Summon Clay Golem directly on the boss. It slows the boss 25% on every hit — stacks with Decrepify for near-immobile bosses.
3
Position Army
Teleport your army close to the boss. Stay at maximum range yourself — let skeletons tank and attack. Refresh Decrepify when it expires.
4
Corpse Explosion on Boss Minions
When boss minions die use CE on their corpses — the explosion radius can hit the boss itself for massive physical/fire damage.

⚠️ PHYSICAL IMMUNE ENEMIES

1
Amplify Damage Curse
Amplify Damage removes Physical Immunity entirely (reduces resistance by 100%). Most "Physical Immune" monsters go from immune to 0% resistance. Use this first.
2
Skeletal Mages
If Amplify Damage doesn't work (some monsters have >100% phys resist), rely on Skeletal Mages. They deal fire, cold, lightning and poison damage — always effective.
3
Lower Resist + Mages
For elemental immune enemies — cast Lower Resist to reduce their elemental resistances, then let Mages deal elemental damage. Handles most situations.

Pro Tips
FCR Breakpoints are crucial: Corpse Explosion and Teleport (Enigma) cast faster at specific Faster Cast Rate thresholds. Target: 125% FCR (fastest Teleport) or at minimum 75% FCR. Spirit Shield (+35%) + Trang Gloves (+20%) + Arachnid Mesh (+20%) + gear = easy to reach.
Amplify Damage first, always: Every combat starts with Amplify Damage on the largest group. It doubles all physical damage from all 30+ minions simultaneously. This is the highest DPS action you can perform every single fight.
Corpse Explosion chains: CE deals damage based on the corpse's max life — not your damage. Larger monsters = bigger explosions. Always trigger CE on the largest corpse first to maximize the chain reaction radius.
Teleport army management: When your army gets scattered (after Teleporting repeatedly), stand still for 2–3 seconds. Skeletons pathfind back to you. Or Teleport to a new position — skeletons "snap" to your new location within seconds.
Iron Golem from Insight: In endgame, replace Clay Golem with Iron Golem made from a spare Insight Runeword polearm. The Iron Golem inherits the Meditation Aura from Insight — giving you double aura stacking if your merc also has Insight.
