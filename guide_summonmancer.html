Build Overview

Summonmancer Fishymancer runs up to 44 minions simultaneously (with Revives). Raise Skeleton builds the primary army. Amplify Damage curse makes all physical-immune monsters vulnerable and doubles minion damage.

Corpse Explosion is the AoE nuke — deals 70–120% of a corpse's max life as physical/fire damage in a large radius. One click can clear a full screen. Enigma runeword gives Teleport to reposition the entire army instantly — the true endgame power spike.

Playstyle & Approach

You are a general, not a soldier. Your army does everything — you manage curses and position.
1
Cast Amplify Damage first
Cast on the largest enemy group immediately. Removes Physical Immunity, doubles all minion damage on affected enemies.
2
One Corpse Explosion click
Find the first corpse (usually from a Golem sacrifice or Clay Golem death) and Corpse Explosion. Chain reaction clears the screen.
3
Cast Decrepify on bosses
Switch from Amplify Damage to Decrepify for all boss fights. Slows 50%, reduces damage 50%, reduces physical resist 50%.
4
Teleport (Enigma) for repositioning
Teleport to reposition your entire army instantly. This is why Enigma is mandatory — your army follows you.
5
Revive strong monsters
Revive Might Aura monsters (e.g. Wendigos) for free aura buffs on all your skeletons. Up to 18 Revives with +skills gear.
09

Pro Tips

💀 Amplify Damage First — Always
Cast Amplify Damage before anything else in every fight. Physical Immunity = Physical Resistance above 100%. Amplify drops it by 100% instantly. Never skip this cast.
⭐ Enigma is Non-Negotiable
Without Enigma (Teleport) your army gets stuck on geometry constantly. It's what makes the build truly S-tier. Save Ber runes — this is the #1 farming target.
🐟 Why 'Fishymancer'?
The nickname comes from Act 2 mercenary + Insight = Meditation aura, combined with the large army. The Meditation aura causes the mana bar to regenerate like fish swimming — always full.
🗡 Beast on a Berserker Axe
Use a Berserker Axe as the Beast base — highest damage fast attack weapon. Indestructible runes in Beast cover the durability. This gives your skeletons a Fanaticism aura from your CHARACTER, not the merc.
