Build Overview

Arsenal Mercenary is a priority-based rotation class. Tracer Missile builds Tracer Lock (5 stacks) → enables Heatseeker Missiles (highest damage) + Rail Shot. Supercharged Gas at 10 stacks opens a 15-second burst window.

Mandalorian Fantasy: Tracer Missile = wrist-mounted rocket. Heatseeker = shoulder-launched homing rockets. Rail Shot = precision blaster execution. Blazing Bolts = dual-blaster suppression fire. Hydraulic Overrides = jetpack sprint.

✓ Strengths
Heavy Armor — tanky enough to solo most content
Dual blasters + rockets = perfect Mando fantasy
Strong burst DPS via Heatseeker + Rail Shot
Most abilities instant-cast or mobile-friendly
Kolto Overload: emergency self-heal
✗ Weaknesses
Tracer Missile requires standing still
Heat management — overheat kills DPS
Lower sustained DPS vs. Innovative Ordnance spec
Difficult to fully optimize at top-end Ops

07

Pro Tips

🎯 Accuracy First, Always
110% Accuracy is non-negotiable. Every missed ability is a wasted GCD. Until you hit 110% your DPS is artificially capped. Ignore all other stats until done.
❄ Vent Heat at 50 — Not 80
Use Vent Heat proactively at 50 Heat. Waiting until 80+ costs more DPS than the extra ability you squeezed in. Heat discipline separates good Arsenal from great.
⚡ Power Surge Timing
Save Power Surge for Tracer Missile casts during Supercharged Gas burst windows. Instant Tracer Missile during burst = maximum DPS uptime. Never waste it on Blazing Bolts.
🚀 Priming Shot on Cooldown
Many Arsenal players forget Priming Shot. It applies an armor debuff AND gives you an instant Tracer Missile cast. Use it every time — significant DPS gain.
