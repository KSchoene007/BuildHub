🪖 "This is the Way." The Arsenal Mercenary is the closest you can get to playing as the Mandalorian in SWTOR. Dual blasters, wrist rockets, jetpack charges, homing missiles and Tracer Missiles — you are a walking weapons platform. Heavy armor, strong defensives and complete self-sufficiency make you the ideal Solo warrior.

Core Mechanic: Arsenal generates Supercharge by firing abilities — at 10 stacks activate Supercharged Gas for a 15-second damage window. Tracer Missile is the primary filler, applying the Tracer Lock debuff (stacks to 5) which enables your hardest-hitting abilities: Heatseeker Missiles and Rail Shot.

Mandalorian Fantasy: Tracer Missile = wrist-mounted rocket. Heatseeker Missiles = shoulder-launched homing rockets. Rail Shot = precision blaster execution. Blazing Bolts = sustained dual-blaster suppression fire. Jet Boost = jetpack knockback. Hydraulic Overrides = jetpack sprint.

Pro Tips
Accuracy First, Always: 110% Accuracy is non-negotiable. Every missed ability is a wasted GCD. Until you hit 110% your DPS is artificially capped — ignore Crit and Alacrity until this is done.
Never Overheat: Use Vent Heat proactively at 50 Heat — never wait until 80+. A GCD lost to overheating costs more DPS than the extra ability you squeezed in. Heat discipline separates good Arsenal players from great ones.
Power Surge Timing: Save Power Surge for Tracer Missile casts during Supercharged Gas burst windows — instant Tracer Missile during burst = maximum DPS uptime. Never waste it on Blazing Bolts.
Priming Shot on Cooldown: Many Arsenal players forget Priming Shot. It applies an armor debuff AND gives you an instant Tracer Missile cast. Use it every time it's available — it's a significant DPS gain.
Outfit Designer for the Look: In SWTOR your appearance is completely separate from your gear stats via the Outfit Designer. You can look exactly like the Mandalorian with silver Beskar armor while wearing your fully optimized endgame gear underneath. Cosmetics cost nothing in combat performance.
Best Content: Story + Veteran Flashpoints, Story Operations, Solo Heroics, Open World. Arsenal is very comfortable for all Solo content and accessible Operations. For Veteran/Master Operations the rotation demands are higher but the class carries well in groups.

Ability Tree · All 8 Choices
ℹ SWTOR 7.6+ Ability Tree System: There are no longer utility points or talent trees with individual points. Instead you make 8 binary Choices — each Choice unlocks at a specific level and offers you 1 of 2–3 options. These replace what was previously the "Utility" system. Most of your abilities are automatically granted as you level — the Choices below are the decisions you actively make.
1
Choice 1 · Unlocks ~Level 10
Mobility Enhancement
✅ Power Overrides (RECOMMENDED)
Reduces cooldown of Concussive Missile and Power Surge by 15 seconds each. Power Surge now grants 2 charges — your next TWO abilities with activation time cast instantly. Essential for the rotation — double instant Tracer Missiles during burst windows.
⬜ Torque Boosters
Hydraulic Overrides duration +4 seconds (12s total). Only useful in fights with lots of forced movement. Power Overrides is almost always better.
🪖 Mandalorian Reason: Double-instant Tracer Missile with Power Surge = rapid-fire wrist rockets. That is the Mandalorian way.
2
Choice 2 · Unlocks ~Level 14
Heat Management
✅ Gyroscopic Alignment Jets (RECOMMENDED)
Recharge 20 Heat when stunned, immobilized or knocked down. Next Tech ability deals +10% damage. Situational but very useful — converts enemy CC into Heat recovery and a damage spike. Strong in content with frequent stuns.
⬜ Jet Escape
Reduces Rocket Out cooldown. Useful for heavy movement content but generally less valuable than Heat management.
🪖 Mandalorian Reason: Getting stunned and immediately recovering Heat while dealing a power spike — exactly how Mando shrugs off enemy attacks and keeps fighting.
3
Choice 3 · Unlocks ~Level 20
AoE Potential
✅ Boresights (RECOMMENDED for Solo / AoE)
+25% damage to Sweeping Blasters. Sweeping Blasters is your primary AoE — this makes it a genuine damage tool in fights with 3+ enemies. Excellent for Heroics and Flashpoints with large trash packs.
⬜ Degauss
Removes one movement-impairing effect when activating Hydraulic Overrides. Situational — only useful in specific Operations encounters. Boresights is better for general content.
🪖 Mandalorian Reason: Sweeping Blasters = spinning dual-blaster sweep through enemy groups. With Boresights this deals real damage — pure Mandalorian crowd control aesthetics.
4
Choice 4 · Unlocks ~Level 26
Burst Damage Enhancement
✅ Acquisitions (RECOMMENDED)
Heatseeker Missiles resets the cooldown on Blazing Bolts. Since Heatseeker is used on cooldown this gives Blazing Bolts significantly more uptime — direct sustained DPS increase. Strong in all content types.
⬜ Stabilizers
Increases the duration of Responsive Safeguards. Defensive option — only take if you are having serious survival issues. Acquisitions is better in almost all situations.
🪖 Why this matters: More Blazing Bolts = more dual-blaster suppression fire uptime. Heatseeker → instant Blazing Bolts reset → continuous fire. This is the core DPS loop.
5
Choice 5 · Unlocks ~Level 34
Survivability
✅ Supercharged Defense (RECOMMENDED)
Activating Supercharged Gas grants 25% AoE damage reduction for 15 seconds. Since Supercharged Gas is used frequently this gives near-permanent AoE DR in multi-target fights. Mandatory in Operations with heavy AoE damage phases.
⬜ Kolto Residue
Kolto Overload heals you for an additional small amount. Minor upgrade to your emergency heal — generally weaker than passive AoE DR in group content.
🪖 Mandalorian Reason: Beskar armor deflects everything. Supercharged Defense = the armor plating activating when Mando goes into burst mode. Thematic AND practical.
6
Choice 6 · Unlocks ~Level 42
Resource Management
✅ Hired Muscle (RECOMMENDED)
Increases the healing received from Kolto Overload and reduces its cooldown by 30 seconds. Your emergency heal becomes more reliable and more frequent — significant quality-of-life improvement for Solo content and reactive survival in Operations.
⬜ Afterburners
Grants an additional charge of Rocket Out. Useful in very mobile fights but generally weaker than improved survivability from Hired Muscle.
🪖 Solo Tip: For Solo Heroics and Story content Hired Muscle is essential — Kolto Overload on a shorter cooldown means you can take on much tougher enemies comfortably without a healer.
7
Choice 7 · Unlocks ~Level 52 · Arsenal-Specific
Discipline Enhancement A · Blazing Bolts
✅ Barrage Surge (RECOMMENDED)
When Blazing Bolts resets via the Barrage passive, the next Blazing Bolts channel also deals +20% damage. Since Blazing Bolts resets every 8 seconds via Barrage this is effectively a permanent +20% damage buff on your highest-damage channel ability. Significant DPS increase.
⬜ Rapid Discharge
Reduces the channel time of Blazing Bolts slightly. Theoretically better for movement-heavy fights but in practice Barrage Surge provides more total damage output.
⭐ This is the most important DPS choice. Barrage Surge directly amplifies your highest-damage ability by 20% every 8 seconds. Never skip this — it is the core of Arsenal's burst potential.
8
Choice 8 · Unlocks ~Level 64 · Arsenal-Specific
Discipline Enhancement B · Heatseeker Missiles
✅ Seeking Missiles (RECOMMENDED)
Heatseeker Missiles fires an additional missile to up to 3 nearby enemies affected by your Heat Signature (Tracer Missile debuff). Single-target: no change. 2–4 targets: Heatseeker becomes a powerful cleave ability. Excellent for Flashpoints and multi-target Operations encounters.
⬜ Thermal Catalyst
Heatseeker Missiles reduces Heat by a small amount. Minor Heat management benefit. Seeking Missiles provides more DPS in virtually all situations with more than one target.
🚀 Mandalorian Reason: Shoulder-launched homing missiles that track MULTIPLE enemies simultaneously. This is Mando firing volleys of Beskar-tipped rockets at an entire squad. Visually spectacular and practically strong.
