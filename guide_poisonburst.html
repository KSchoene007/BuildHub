✗ Weaknesses
✗Mana management important early
✗Needs a good bow as main weapon

✗Plaguefinger unique for optimal scaling

Core Mechanic: Poison scales with Physical + Chaos Hit Damage. Overwhelming Toxicity (Ascendancy) doubles the maximum Poison stack count on targets. Herald of Plague automatically spreads the strongest active Poison to nearby enemies. Herald of Blood triggers via Bleed (from Bursting Plague) to fire chain explosions — that's the "one-button" clear mechanism.

⚑ You can play Poison from Level 1 — no build swap needed! Poisonburst Arrow is your main skill from start to finish.

Rotation & Playstyle

🗺 MAPPING (Standard)

Simply shoot Poisonburst Arrow into packs. Herald of Plague automatically spreads the strongest Poison. Bursting Plague triggers Bleed → Herald of Blood explodes → chain explosions offscreen. In endgame: one-button clear, no further actions needed.

⚔ BOSSES (Standard)

1
Pre-place Vine Arrow
Place 3× Vine Arrow on the boss position before engaging. Slows + applies DoT + gets poisoned.
2
Gas Arrow
Throw Gas Arrow onto the boss. Creates gas cloud for Toxic Growth detonations.
3
Toxic Growth spam
Spam Toxic Growth into the gas cloud until it disperses. Each pustule stores massive poison damage.
4
Repeat Gas Arrow
Throw Gas Arrow again → continue Toxic Growth spam.
5
Poisonburst Arrow
Use to refresh Poison stacks between Toxic Growth casts.

👑 PINNACLE BOSSES

1
Stormcaller Arrow + Voltaic Mark
~100% Shock on boss (lasts 30 seconds). Massive damage multiplier for the entire fight.
2
Despair Curse
Lower the boss's Chaos resistance before beginning the damage rotation.
3
Withering Presence
Swap in instead of Herald of Blood for Wither stacks → more Chaos Damage taken by boss.
4–5
Normal boss loop
Execute the standard Gas → Vine → Toxic Growth spam rotation. Shock doesn't need to be renewed — boss will die before it expires.

Pro Tips
Bow quality is the biggest damage factor. Always prioritize high Physical Damage as the primary stat on your bow. Other elemental damage types only help once you have Plaguefinger equipped.
Mana Management: Use Ultimate Mana Flask with "Increased Mana Recovered". Optional: Considered Casting (3 passive points) for massive mana cost reduction. Alternative: Physical Mana Leech on rings or gloves.
Vendor Regex for shopping: (10|15)% i.+mov|ph.*da|s: bow 1
Stat Priorities when gearing:
1. High Physical Damage Bow  ·  2. Rigwald's Ferocity (Slot 2)  ·  3. Evasion Rating (Ghost Dance)  ·  4. Chaos Res ≥ 30–50%  ·  5. Elemental Res cap (75%)  ·  6. Phys Mana Leech  ·  7. Plaguefinger endgame

Patch Notes · 0.5.0 Impact
⚠️ Patch 0.5.0 — Return of the Ancients: The Poisonburst Pathfinder received significant nerfs to Poison Duration. The build remains viable but requires adjustments to compensate. Read carefully before playing.
🔴 NERF · Poisonburst Arrow
Poison Duration: Now 3 seconds at all Gem levels
Previously scaled with Gem level. 3 sec flat duration means less total damage per stack at higher levels. Compensate via +Poison Duration on gear and passive tree. Stack as much Poison Duration as possible to offset this nerf.
🔴 NERF · Pathfinder Ascendancy — Overwhelming Toxicity
50% less Poison Duration (previously 35%)
This is the biggest nerf. Combined with the Poisonburst Arrow duration nerf, total effective Poison Duration is significantly shorter. Priority fix: invest heavily in +Poison Duration nodes on the passive tree and prioritize Poison Duration on jewels and gear.
🟡 MINOR NERF · Pathfinder — Running Assault
30% less Movement Speed Penalty (previously 50%)
Minor mobility nerf. The build is still mobile but slightly less fluid when using skills while moving. Not build-breaking.
🟢 NEUTRAL · Leech Cap Change
Max 40,000 damage considered for Leech calculation
If the build relied on Leech for survivability via high hit damage, this caps the Leech benefit from large hits. Mostly irrelevant for a DoT Poison build since Leech is typically tied to hit damage, not DoT.
💡 How to adapt post-0.5.0:
1. Prioritize +Poison Duration on all gear slots — rings, amulet, jewels
2. Invest passive tree points into Poison Duration nodes near the Ranger/Pathfinder area
3. Consider Anomalous Poisonburst Arrow if available for duration quality bonus
4. The build is still S-Tier for map clear — single target boss DPS is reduced but manageable
5. Apply more stacks per fight by staying engaged longer — maintain maximum hit rate
