Build Overview

Poisonburst Pathfinder is an S-Tier Poison DoT Ranger — playable from Level 1 to endgame with the same skill. No build swap needed.

Core Mechanic: Poison scales with Physical + Chaos Hit. Overwhelming Toxicity doubles max Poison stacks. Herald of Plague spreads strongest Poison to nearby enemies. Herald of Blood triggers chain explosions via Bleed — one-button map clear.

09

Pro Tips

☣ Poison Stack Rate
Every hit adds a new stack. Overwhelming Toxicity doubles the cap. Focus on rapid hits, not single big hits — fire rate > single-hit damage.
🌊 Herald of Plague Timing
Build high stacks on one target first. Herald then spreads the strongest one to all nearby — clears entire screens after a single kill.
⚡ Stormcaller for Pinnacle
Switch to Stormcaller Arrow + Voltaic Mark for Pinnacle bosses. The 100% Shock multiplier is a massive DPS increase on high-HP targets.
💰 Budget to Endgame
Fully self-found from Level 1. Plaguefinger is the first major gear gate — everything else is secondary. Cheapest S-tier build in PoE2.
