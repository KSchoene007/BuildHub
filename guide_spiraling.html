Core Mechanic: Spiraling Conspiracy is a Level-18 Persistent Minion Skill, exclusively granted by The Raven's Flock. The flock counts as a single collective minion — all Minion Damage bonuses, Minion Life bonuses, and Chaos DoT multipliers scale it. The staff also grants 111% increased Minion Damage and 34% Spirit Reservation Efficiency.

Mad Flight is the active burst command: all ravens fly between two mirror illusions and deal massive damage in a corridor. Use for bosses and elite packs.

⚠ The Raven's Flock (and thus Spiraling Conspiracy) is only usable from Level 18. Until then you play as a classic Minion Witch with Skeletal Warriors + Raging Spirits.

Rotation & Playstyle

🗺 MAPPING (Standard)

Simply run through the map — the ravens circle permanently and automatically damage everything in range. Place Flame Wall briefly while running for the Arsonist buff. Bone Cage for dense packs. No active attack needed except occasional Mad Flight for Elites.

⚔ BOSSES (Standard)

1
Flame Wall
Place Flame Wall on the boss position.
2
Pain Offering
Activate for the Minion Damage buff before Mad Flight.
3
Mad Flight
Activate → all ravens fly corridor burst onto boss.
4
Wait for return
Wait for ravens to return → repeat Mad Flight. Use Bone Cage if boss moves too fast.
5
Arsonists via Flame Wall
Keep shooting Skeletal Arsonists through Flame Wall for additional burst.

👑 PINNACLE BOSSES

1
Flammability Curse
Apply to lower fire resistance before the fight.
2
Enfeeble (optional)
Second curse for damage reduction on the boss.
3
Swap Withering Presence
Swap in Withering Presence Spirit Gem for Wither stacks on boss.
4
Mad Flight rotation
Execute Mad Flight rotation with short rest phases between bursts.
⚠ Raven Flock Note: The raven flock grows with kills and the longer you stay near Unique enemies. In Tier 15+ maps: wait briefly at the entrance until the flock reaches maximum size from the first kills, then approach the boss.

Pro Tips
The Raven's Flock is the most important item. Without it, Spiraling Conspiracy doesn't exist. Trade for it or farm it as your absolute first priority. There is no alternative.
Stat Priorities when gearing:
1. Get The Raven's Flock  ·  2. +1 Minion Skill Level on amulet or helm  ·  3. Spirit Reservation Efficiency  ·  4. Chaos Res ≥ 60%  ·  5. Max Energy Shield  ·  6. Cap elemental resistances (75%)  ·  7. Intelligence for skill requirements
Raven Flock Growth: The flock size grows with kills. In T15+ maps wait at the entrance to let the flock reach max size before pushing to the boss — this significantly increases Mad Flight burst damage.
Best Notable Targets: Minion Damage (all available) · Essence Infusion (ES Recharge) · Spirit Reservation Efficiency · Chaos / DoT Multiplier · Area of Effect (raven radius) · Minion Life · Maximum Energy Shield · Jewel Sockets (×2 minimum)

Patch Notes · 0.5.0 Impact
✅ Patch 0.5.0 — Return of the Ancients: Good news for Minion builds! The Spiraling Conspiracy Infernalist received indirect buffs through new Minion-specific content, a new Passive Cluster, and better Minion management. No direct nerfs.
🟢 BUFF · New Passive Cluster
Archon of Undeath — New Witch/Sorceress Cluster
A new Archon of Undeath cluster has been added to the Witch/Sorceress area of the Passive Tree. Directly relevant for Minion/Undeath builds — invest points here for additional Minion bonuses. Check the cluster on the Passive Tree and prioritize relevant nodes.
🟢 BUFF · New Support Gems
Minion Splash + Minion Splash II
Two new Strength Support Gems for Minions that Strike — grants Melee Splash to those Minion strikes. If Spiraling Conspiracy uses any melee Minions these supports add AoE clear. Test in your gem setup for potential DPS gain on trash packs.
🟢 BUFF · Minion Management
Younger Minions removed first on weapon swap
When Minions are removed due to resource changes (weapon swapping), newer Minions are now removed first instead of older ones. This prevents wasting Minion cooldown abilities unnecessarily — quality of life improvement for all Minion builds.
🟢 BUFF · New Crafting Options
Minion mods now available on Rings and Belts via Genesis Tree
The new Genesis Tree (Breach mechanic) can craft Minion-specific mods onto Rings and Belts. Previously Minion mods were restricted to certain slot types. This significantly expands gear optimization options for the build.
🟡 NEUTRAL · Leech Cap Change
Max 40,000 damage per hit for Leech calculation
Minion builds don't typically rely on personal Leech so this change is largely irrelevant. If your Minions have Leech passives, the cap only affects very high single-hit damage values above 40K.
💡 What to update post-0.5.0:
1. Check the new Archon of Undeath passive cluster — invest points here
2. Test Minion Splash Support Gems on melee Minion skills for AoE improvement
3. Farm Breach content for the Genesis Tree to craft Minion mods on Rings/Belts
4. Overall: build gets stronger in 0.5.0 — no adaptation required, only optimization
