Core Mechanic

⚠ Level 18 required. Spiraling Conspiracy only works with The Raven's Flock equipped. Until Level 18: classic Minion Witch (Skeletal Warriors + Raging Spirits).

Spiraling Conspiracy summons a permanent raven flock that orbits you, dealing Chaos DoT to everything in range. The flock acts as a single collective minion — all Minion Damage bonuses and Chaos DoT multipliers scale it. The staff grants +111% Minion Damage and 34% Spirit Reservation Efficiency.

Mad Flight is the burst command — all ravens fly between two mirror illusions dealing massive corridor damage. Always use after Pain Offering for maximum output. The raven flock grows larger with kills — in T15+ maps wait at the entrance before engaging the boss.

Patch 0.5.0 — Return of the Ancients

⚠️ Return of the Ancients (May 29, 2026): Spiraling Conspiracy (Infernalist Minion) received a significant nerf via Infernal Legion I support gem. If you are using Infernal Legion as a primary damage mechanic, this build requires adaptation. Core minion damage is otherwise unaffected.
🔴 Infernal Legion I Nerfed
Now deals 10% max Life as Fire damage/sec (was 20%). If Infernal Legion was your primary damage scaler, swap to Herald of Ice Infernalist variant or Twisted Imperium setup. Do NOT pursue Infernal Legion as your main scaling mechanic in 0.5.
🔴 Energy Shield Overhaul
ES defenses took a significant hit system-wide. Infernalist suffers more than other classes. Compensate with Runic Ward via Verisium Runeforging on armours — new in 0.5. Prism Guardian builds need rethinking.
🔴 Living Lightning Changed
No longer replaces minions when summoning beyond maximum. Adjust your summoning rotation accordingly — you can no longer "cycle" minions to refresh buffs.
🟢 Core Minion Damage Unchanged
Skeletal Warriors, Raging Spirits, and Spectres base damage not nerfed. The standard Army Infernalist (Volcano → Raging Spirits → Unearth) remains strong. Replace Flame Wall with Volcano for easier ignite application in the campaign.
🟢 Runic Ward — New Defense Layer
Use Verisium to add Runic Ward to armours. Starting Act 3, Unique Armours can be Runeforged. This partially compensates the ES nerf for defensive scaling on Infernalist.
🟢 Skeletal Warrior Supports Updated
Use Amanamu's Tithe for Skeletal Warrior supports (replaces previous setup). Significant DPS improvement in the new patch.
🟢 Minion Splash Gems Added
New Minion Splash I & II Strength Support Gems added. Creates minions which Strike with Melee Splash. Extra AoE for your army — excellent addition for this build.
🟢 New League: Runes of Aldur
60+ new Runes added. Destroying Unique items creates Runes with their properties. Massive crafting upgrade potential for minion gear. Farm Uniques → destroy → imprint into your best items.
🟡 Atlas Overhaul
Atlas rebuilt around fixed points of interest. New Fortress structure replaces old Atlas passive point system. Respec your Atlas tree on league start — old layouts are obsolete.
🟡 Recommended Build Variant
Community consensus: switch to Herald of Ice Infernalist (Twisted Imperium) as the optimal 0.5 Infernalist league starter. Spiraling Conspiracy remains viable if not relying on Infernal Legion, but top tier is now B→A with Infernal Legion gutted.
✅ Return of the Ancients: Spiraling Conspiracy received indirect buffs. No direct nerfs. Build gets stronger in 0.5.0 — no adaptation required, only optimization.
🟢 New Passive Cluster — Archon of Undeath
New cluster in Witch/Sorceress area. Invest here after core Minion Damage nodes.
🟢 New Support Gems — Minion Splash I & II
Test on Skeletal Arsonists for potential AoE improvement on trash packs.
🟢 Minion Management QoL
Younger Minions removed first on weapon swap. Prevents wasting Minion cooldowns.
🟢 Genesis Tree Crafting
Minion mods craftable on Rings and Belts via Genesis Tree (Breach mechanic).
🟡 Leech Cap — Neutral
Max 40,000 dmg per hit for Leech. Irrelevant for Minion DoT builds.

Pro Tips

The Raven's Flock is Everything
Without it, Spiraling Conspiracy doesn't exist. Farm or trade for it as your absolute first priority. There is no alternative.
Raven Flock Size Matters
The flock grows with kills. In T15+ maps wait at entrance for max flock size before the boss — this dramatically increases Mad Flight burst damage.
Stat Priority Order
1. The Raven's Flock · 2. +1 Minion Skill Level · 3. Spirit Reservation Efficiency · 4. Chaos Res ≥60% · 5. Max Energy Shield · 6. Ele Res caps (75%) · 7. Intelligence
Best Notable Targets
Minion Damage (all) · Essence Infusion · Spirit Reservation Efficiency · Chaos/DoT Multiplier · Area of Effect · Minion Life · Max Energy Shield · Jewel Sockets ×2 minimum
