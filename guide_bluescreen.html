Build Overview

The Bluescreen build combines the Exotic Bluescreen LMG with the new Tipping Scales gear set (introduced in Y8S1). The Bluescreen's Disruptor Rounds talent marks every enemy hit and builds stacks — at 50 stacks the detonation disorients and disrupts all marked enemies in a 10-meter radius simultaneously.

Playstyle: Sustained LMG fire, build Disruptor stacks quickly, detonate on demand via skill use or enemy kill. The Tipping Scales set amplifies weapon damage multiplicatively with every kill — making this build snowball extremely hard in dense enemy groups.

How to get Bluescreen: Guaranteed first drop from completing the Jefferson Trade Center SHD Mission (Manhunt: Captain Lewis). After that available from Exotic Caches and LMG Targeted Loot.

Frenzy + Measured
This talent combo is the key: Frenzy activates at the start of a magazine (+15% WD + Fire Rate), Measured reduces recoil and maintains damage at the end. Ideal magazine size: ~300 rounds so Frenzy runs out exactly when the magazine empties — keeping both talents permanently active.
Magazine Size is critical: The secondary LMG should have a magazine of around 300 rounds. Too large = Frenzy inactive period in the middle. Too small = too many reloads. ~300 is the sweet spot for permanent Frenzy + Measured uptime.

Pro Tips
Disruptor Timing: Don't always wait for 50 stacks. In dense groups kill any marked enemy immediately to chain-detonate — the AoE hits everything nearby. In single target fights build to 50 first for maximum burst.
Magazine Management: The secondary LMG with Frenzy + Measured needs ~300 rounds. Time your reloads so Frenzy activates at the start of every magazine — never reload mid-Frenzy unless absolutely necessary.
Blood Sucker Stacking: With Gunner's +10% Armor on Kill and Blood Sucker (+3% per kill), your armor ramps up very fast in groups. Don't play too passively — the kills are what keep you alive.
No Shield Skills! Always use Reviver Hive or Decoy — never Crusader Shield or Ballistic Shield. Shield skills explicitly do not trigger Disruptor Rounds detonation.
Best Content: Legendary Missions (excellent in groups), The Summit, Countdown, Heroic Strongholds. The build snowballs hard — the more enemies in a room the stronger it gets.
Tinkerer Armor: Keep your total armor above 1.1M for Blatza to fire at full efficiency. If you drop below 1.1M the talent loses significant value — adjust mods accordingly.
