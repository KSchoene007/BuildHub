Build Overview

The Bluescreen build combines the Exotic Bluescreen LMG with the new Tipping Scales gear set. The Disruptor Rounds talent marks every enemy hit and builds stacks — at 50 stacks the detonation disorients all marked enemies in a 10-meter AoE radius simultaneously.

Playstyle: Sustained LMG fire, build Disruptor stacks quickly, detonate on demand via skill use or kill. Tipping Scales amplifies weapon damage multiplicatively with every kill — snowballing hard in dense groups. How to get Bluescreen: Guaranteed first drop from Jefferson Trade Center SHD Mission (Manhunt: Captain Lewis).

✓ Strengths
Guaranteed first drop from Jefferson TC
CC + DPS in 10m AoE simultaneously
Tipping Scales snowballs hard in groups
Frenzy + Measured on secondary = near-100% uptime
✗ Weaknesses

Pro Tips

💥 Disruptor Timing
Don't always wait for 50 stacks. In dense groups kill any marked enemy immediately for chain detonation. In single target fights build to 50 first for maximum burst.
🔫 Magazine Management
The secondary LMG needs ~300 rounds. Time reloads so Frenzy activates at the start of every magazine — never reload mid-Frenzy unless necessary.
🌾 Farming Bluescreen
Guaranteed first drop: Jefferson Trade Center SHD Mission. After that: LMG Targeted Loot missions on Challenging+ or Heroic, Exotic Caches from seasonal events.
⚠ No Shields Ever
Shield skills do not trigger Disruptor detonation. Reviver Hive and Decoy are the correct skill choices — they both trigger it on use.
