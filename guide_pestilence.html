Build Overview

The Pestilence build combines the Exotic Pestilence LMG with the Negotiator's Dilemma gear set to simultaneously mark multiple enemies with harmful DoT (Damage over Time) and chain damage waves between targets.

Ideal for Control Points, The Summit, and Heroic/Legendary missions. Playstyle: Mark enemies with critical hits (up to 3–5 marks), stack Pestilence's viral DoT, chain damage to other targets via the Negotiator effect. Cover-based play recommended.

Pro Tips
Maintain Marks: Always land critical hits to mark enemies (up to 5 simultaneously). Every kill of a marked enemy gives you +2% Crit Damage — that adds up to +40% at 20 kills!
Exploit Pestilence DoT: Keep the DoT effect from Pestilence always active. When a poisoned enemy dies the debuff transfers to nearby enemies — perfect for dense enemy packs.
Use Decoy Tactically: The Decoy skill breaks up enemy groups and allows you to mark multiple enemies simultaneously — maximum synergy with the Negotiator's set.
Coyote's Mask: This exotic mask is Best-in-Slot for this build as it grants additional Crit bonuses for both close and distant enemies. If unavailable, use a red-core mask with Crit stats.
Best Content: Control Points (Heroic), The Summit, Countdown, Heroic/Legendary Missions. For Legendary content use cover-based play for best results.
