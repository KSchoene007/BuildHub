Build Overview

The Pestilence build maximizes Plague of the Outcasts DoT damage through raw Weapon Damage stacking. Every single DoT tick scales directly with your WD stat — so every % of WD you add multiplies all 50 active stacks simultaneously.

Core Synergy: 4× Core Strength cross-scales Armor and WD simultaneously via the 4-piece bonus. Backbone Named Backpack adds up to +35% WD via kill stacks. Overdogs Exotic Gloves provide Crit Chance and Crit Damage. Together this gives you a build that is both tanky enough for Solo Legendary and hits hard enough to melt groups.

Playstyle: Mid-range cover-based. Spray Pestilence to apply DoT stacks → kill one poisoned enemy to chain the DoT to nearby targets → let ticks finish the rest. Use grenades on room entry for 2 instant Backbone stacks. Keep shooting, keep killing, keep stacks active.

Pro Tips
Grenade on Room Entry: Always throw a grenade as you enter a new room. A grenade kill instantly adds 2 Backbone stacks — you enter every fight at near-max WD.
DoT Chain Trigger: Kill ONE poisoned enemy to chain the full stack to nearby enemies. Don't dump all ammo into one target — mark several, kill one, let the chain do the rest.
~1.5M WD Sweet Spot: For Solo play the sweet spot is around 1.5M Weapon Damage. Above that you sacrifice too much armor — the Core Strength 4-piece cross-scaling makes this the ideal balance point.
Decoy for Grouping: Enemies need to be within 25m for the DoT chain to trigger. Use Decoy to pull groups tighter — the tighter they cluster the more simultaneous chains you get.
Cover-Based on Legendary: Pestilence is a passive DoT build — you don't need line of sight after applying the stacks. Duck behind cover, let ticks do the work, pop out to refresh stacks and chain again.
Best Content: Control Points (Heroic), The Summit, Countdown, Heroic + Legendary Missions. Exceptional in dense enemy areas — the more enemies in a room the more DoT chains you trigger simultaneously.
