Build Overview

The Pestilence build maximizes Plague of the Outcasts DoT damage through raw Weapon Damage stacking. Every single DoT tick scales directly with your WD stat — every % of WD multiplies all 50 active stacks simultaneously.

Core Synergy: 4× Core Strength cross-scales Armor and WD simultaneously (75%). Backbone Named Backpack adds up to +35% WD via kill stacks. Overdogs Exotic Gloves provide Crit Chance and Crit Damage. Tanky enough for Solo Legendary while hitting hard enough to melt groups.

✓ Strengths
DoT chains between enemies on kill
Core Strength: WD stacking = Armor stacking
Backbone: up to +35% WD from kills
Pestilence debuff: -50% healing on enemies
Tanky enough for Solo Legendary
✗ Weaknesses
DoT is delayed — not instant kill
Backbone stacks need active kill management
Mid-range cover-based — not highly mobile

09

Pro Tips

💣 Grenade on Entry
Always throw a grenade entering each room. Instantly adds 2 Backbone stacks. You enter every fight at near-max WD. Never skip this.
☣ DoT Chain — Kill One
Kill ONE poisoned enemy to chain the full stack. Don't dump all ammo — mark several, kill one, let the chain cascade across the room.
🎭 Decoy for Grouping
Enemies need to be within 25m for the chain to trigger. Decoy pulls them close before spraying — maximizes chain coverage per kill.
🌾 Farming Priority
Backbone: Season Pass Rank 61 (guaranteed). Overdogs: Exotic Caches / Gloves Targeted Loot. Core Strength: Heroic general loot. Pestilence: Hyena Control Points.
