Build Overview

Ice Shot Deadeye is one of the strongest all-round builds in PoE2 0.5 Return of the Ancients — smoother, tankier, and with better endgame scaling than Lightning Arrow. Ice Shot fires a piercing cold projectile creating ice explosions that freeze entire packs. The 0.5 signature mechanic: Mirage Deadeye now channels Snipe — your clone stands still channeling while you freely move and freeze with Ice Shot. Snipe deals ~3,067% damage on frozen targets and one-shots virtually every boss.
⚠️ Ice Shot unlocks at level 31. Level with Lightning Arrow + Lightning Rod until then — full swap at 31.
✓ Strengths
Mirage Deadeye + Snipe = one-shot bosses while moving freely
Herald of Ice explosions = instant screen-wide clear
Freeze = enemies can't move or attack — best defense in game
Deflect (Evasion-based) adds massive less damage taken layer
No required Uniques — T15+ maps in rares
S-Tier ascendancy — Gathering Winds, Far Shot, Endless Munitions all unchanged
✗ Weaknesses
Ice Shot locked until level 31 — leveling uses Lightning Arrow
25% less freeze buildup in 0.5 — negligible in practice but noticeable early
Chain clear slightly slower than Lightning Arrow
BiS endgame gear (Hyrri's Ire, Cadiro's Gambit) requires farming

Core Mechanic — Wie der Build wirklich funktioniert

🔴 Korrektur: Mirage Deadeye channelt NICHT automatisch Snipe. Beide Mirages (Archer + Deadeye) laufen Ice Shot. Snipe ist dein manueller Boss-Finisher.

1. Ice Shot trifft Pack → Kalt-Explosion → Freeze
2. Mirage Archer + Mirage Deadeye feuern ebenfalls Ice Shot → mehr Freeze
3. Herald of Ice triggert auf Shatters → Screen-Kette
4. Tornado Shot zusätzliche AoE-Abdeckung
⚔ Boss Rotation
1. Weapon Set 2 → Freezing Mark auf Boss
2. Weapon Set 1 → Ice Shot → Boss einfrieren
3. Boss eingefroren → Snipe manuell channeln (nah rangehen!)
4. Icy Blast → ~3.067% Schaden → Boss tot

🧊 Freeze Loop
Ice Shot → Fork → mehrere Hits gleichzeitig → Freeze schneller aufgebaut. Beide Mirages feuern dazu → nahezu sofortiger Freeze auf alle Targets. Freeze ist deine Defense, nicht nur Offense.
⚡ Frenzy Charges
Barrage generiert Frenzy Charges über Perpetual Charge. Combat Frenzy verbraucht sie für massiven Damage Burst. Wichtig gegen Bosse — vor Snipe aufbauen.
💡 Snipe Timing Tipp von Fubgun: 5–10 Minuten Übung nötig. Das Timing passt sich an deine Attack Speed an — mit höherem Gear verschiebt es sich leicht. Übe in Maps, nicht erst beim Endgame Boss.

Passive Tree Priority

💡 Ranger starts bottom-right (Dexterity section). Path upward toward Cold Damage and Critical Strike clusters.
① Cold Damage Notables
Highest priority from the start. Every Cold Damage notable multiplies Ice Shot, Herald of Ice explosions, and Snipe Icy Blast simultaneously. Maximum return per point invested.
② Critical Strike Chance + Multiplier
Ice Shot scales extremely well with crits. Path toward the Ranger crit cluster after capping cold damage notables. Crit multiplier directly scales Snipe burst damage.
③ Projectile Speed & Damage
Faster projectiles reach Far Shot range faster — more damage per hit. Also improves Snipe's travel speed. Path through projectile nodes on the way to crit cluster.
④ Attack Speed
More Ice Shot casts per second = more freeze application = more Herald of Ice explosions. Especially important for maintaining Mirage Deadeye uptime and Mana Remnant generation.
⑤ Evasion + Deflect
Deflect (new in 0.5) scales with Evasion rating — invest in Evasion nodes for both dodge chance and massive less damage taken. Pairs with freeze for near-invulnerability.
⑥ Freeze Buildup / Duration
More freeze duration = longer Snipe window = more reliable boss kills. Minor investment — enough to maintain freeze through the Snipe channel duration.
⑦ Resistance Pathing
Cap all resistances (75%) as you progress. Path through resistance nodes naturally on the way to damage clusters.

Pro Tips

Never Channel Snipe Yourself
Mirage Deadeye does it for you. Your job is to keep the boss frozen with Ice Shot while your clone handles the burst. If you find yourself channeling Snipe manually, you're doing it wrong — use Mirage Deadeye as your Snipe engine.
Freeze = Your Best Defense
Frozen enemies cannot move, attack, or cast. In packs, freeze the first enemy and Herald of Ice clears everything. You take 0 incoming attacks during a full freeze clear. Prioritize freeze buildup over raw damage — survivability and DPS are the same stat.
Far Shot: Always Stay at Range
Far Shot gives 30% more damage at full projectile travel distance. Never let enemies get close — use Gathering Winds movement speed to maintain distance and maximize every projectile's damage bonus.
Cadiro's Gambit Changes Everything
When you get Cadiro's Gambit quiver, the build completely transforms. Crescendo, Splinter, Reversing, and Diamond effects mean returning crit arrows that split into cascading projectiles. Gamble at the Purveyor of Curiosities for Quiver slots whenever you have Obols.
Freezing Salvo for Boss Freeze
Some bosses resist freeze buildup. Lead every boss phase with Freezing Salvo to guarantee the initial freeze, then maintain with Ice Shot. Never skip this opening — Snipe without freeze is just a regular shot.
Campaign: Use Lightning Arrow Properly
Before level 31: Lightning Arrow with Chain II spawns 8 Lightning Rods per shot. Use Barrage to stack rods instantly on bosses. This is one of the fastest campaign leveling setups in the game — don't rush to Ice Shot before 31.
Mirage Deadeye Cooldown
If Mirage Deadeye expires during a boss fight, immediately resummon. Your Snipe burst window closes entirely without the Mirage. Keep Cooldown Recovery gear to reduce Mirage Deadeye resummon time.
Herald of Ice Sound Cue
The shattering sound of Herald of Ice is your signal that the pack is dead. If you hear the chain shatters, keep moving — don't stop to attack enemies that are already dying. Trust the chain and push forward.
