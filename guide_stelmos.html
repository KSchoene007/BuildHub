Build Overview

St. Elmo's Engine is an Exotic SMG with the unique Actum Est talent: every hit generates +1 stack — at 100 stacks the next magazine loads with shock rounds that stun enemies and deal additional damage.

Playstyle: The build combines sustained high damage (Striker's Gamble stacks), survivability (Memento), and shock effects (Actum Est) for unmatched efficiency across all difficulty levels. With a 70+1 round magazine it has the largest magazine of any AR/SMG in the game.

4× Striker's Battlegear · Core Mechanic
Striker's Gamble
Every hit stacks +0.5% Weapon Damage (max 50 stacks = +25% multiplicative). With the 70+1 magazine, stacks build blazingly fast — this is the core DPS mechanic of the build.

Obliterate
Every critical hit increases Weapon Damage by 1% (additive). Brand Piece chest — talent is freely choosable. Works directly with Striker's Gamble stacks for massive sustained DPS.

Pro Tips
Understand the Shock Cycle: The 70-round magazine means you automatically collect 100 hits in 1–2 mags. After reloading you immediately have the shock magazine — no manual effort required.
Hold Striker Stacks: The large magazine makes it easier to maintain 50 Striker's Gamble stacks. Keep shooting continuously, don't interrupt — the damage bonus stacks multiplicatively.
Thunder Strike Synergy: With Thunder Strike as a secondary talent you get +30–40% damage on stunned enemies. Since shock rounds stun enemies, this bonus is essentially permanent during shock magazines.
Collect Memento Trophies: Always pick up Kill Confirmed trophies! The long-term buff (+30% Weapon Damage for 5 minutes) makes this build even stronger — and it's easy to maintain.
⚠️ Group Note: If a teammate has Ongoing Directive active, their Hollow-Point ammo will override St. Elmo's shock rounds. Be aware of this in group play!
