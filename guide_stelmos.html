Build Overview

St. Elmo's Engine is an Exotic SMG with the unique Actum Est talent: every hit generates +1 stack — at 100 stacks the next magazine loads with shock rounds that stun enemies and deal additional damage.

Playstyle: Combines sustained high damage (Striker's Gamble stacks), survivability (Memento), and shock effects for unmatched efficiency. With a 70+1 round magazine — the largest of any AR/SMG in the game.

Pro Tips

⚡ Shock Rounds Timing
Save the 100-stack shock magazine for dense elite packs or boss phases. The 50% armor pierce makes it devastating when timed well. Don't waste it on trash.
🏆 Memento Management
Farm trash packs before boss rooms to reach 30 kills. Enter every boss encounter with full Kill Confirmed (+30% WD + Armor Regen) active for maximum burst.
🔫 Secondary Weapon Choice
Thunder Strike on your secondary pairs perfectly with shock rounds — +30–40% damage on stunned enemies. Switch to secondary when enemies are stunned from the shock mag.
🎯 Best Content
Heroic & Legendary Missions, The Summit, Countdown, all Raids. St. Elmo's + Striker combination is the #1 meta build in 2026 — viable and effective in all PvE content.
