Build Overview

Lightning Arrow Deadeye is the #1 Most Played build in PoE2 — S-Tier league starter with the fastest campaign clear in the game. No gear required to start.

Core Chain Reaction: LA hits → beams arc to 3 nearby enemies → Freeze + Shock → on kill Herald of Thunder triggers → explosion → chain reaction clears entire screens. Plant Lightning Rods before boss phases for devastating burst damage.

✓ Strengths
S-Tier League Starter — no gear required
Fastest campaign clear in the game
Screen-wide clear via chaining lightning beams
Lightning Rod for massive single-target burst
Mirage Deadeye = free copy of your attacks
Reaches red maps on self-found rare gear
✗ Weaknesses
Kite-dependent defense — never stand still
Mana sustain requires investment (Sapphire Jewel)
Lightning Beams no longer chain to same target (0.5 nerf)
High-end bow is expensive

Main skill. Chaining beams to 3 enemies. Supports: Fork · Overpower · Lightning Penetration · Added Lightning Damage

Fires a tornado seeking the target. Use with Voltaic Mark. Supports: Added Lightning · Overpower · Lightning Pen

Kill Shocked enemy with LA → massive lightning explosion. Always active. What makes map clear godlike.

Flat damage reduction on Crit. Deadeye crits constantly = near-permanent 20% less damage taken from Shocked enemies.
👻
Ghost Dance — Defense
Uses Evasion Rating to restore Energy Shield on hit. Essential for survivability without armor.

09

Pro Tips

⚡ Always Be Moving
This is the most important rule. Lightning Aegis and Ghost Dance scale with movement. Stationary targets die. Mobile targets survive.
🔱 Lightning Rod Placement
Pre-place Rods before triggering boss phases. The burst window when the boss enters a Rod zone is where all your DPS happens.
💧 Sapphire Jewel for Mana
Without it you'll constantly interrupt your attack for mana. Get a Sapphire Jewel in a ring or as a passive tree jewel ASAP.
🌩 Herald of Thunder — Always Active
Never replace it. Even on bosses. The explosion chain is what makes map clear work. Keep mana reserved for it always.
