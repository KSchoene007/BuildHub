Build Overview

✓S-Tier League Starter — no gear required to start
✓Fastest campaign clear in the game
✓Screen-wide clear via chaining lightning beams
✓Herald of Thunder chain explosions on kill
✓Lightning Rod for massive single-target burst
✓Surprisingly tanky via Lightning Aegis + Ghost Dance
✓Mirage Deadeye = free copy firing your attacks
✓Reaches red maps on self-found rare gear
✗ Weaknesses

✗High-end gear is expensive (bow-specific stats)

Core Chain Reaction: Lightning Arrow hits → Beams arc to 3 nearby enemies → enemies Freeze + Shock → on kill Herald of Thunder triggers → lightning explosion kills more enemies → chain reaction clears entire screens. Plant Lightning Rods before boss phases → fire Lightning Arrow at Rods → devastating burst damage. Tornado Shot + Voltaic Mark for single-target finishing.

Ascendancy · Deadeye
💡 Deadeye is the only Ascendancy for this build. The synergy between Gathering Winds (constant Crits), Mirage Archer (free copy of your attacks), and Bullseye (10 Critical Weakness stacks) is what makes Lightning Arrow reach S-Tier. Take these in order.
Ascendancy 1 · First Pick
Gathering Winds
+Tailwind on Kill+Action Speed stackingEnables constant Crits
Each kill grants Tailwind (+Action Speed). Stacks rapidly in packs. Combined with Lightning Arrow's chain kills — permanent high action speed by the second enemy group.
Ascendancy 2 · Priority
Mirage Archer
Creates Mirage copyFires your attacks for freeNo passive investment needed
A temporary Mirage Deadeye copy is created that also fires Lightning Arrow. Effectively doubles your projectile output for free. One of the strongest Ascendancy nodes in the game.
Ascendancy 3
Bullseye
10 Critical Weakness stacksDoubles Crit payoffWas 5 stacks in 0.4
Applies 10 Critical Weakness stacks on Crit (buffed from 5 in 0.4). Since Deadeye crits constantly via Gathering Winds, this is a permanent massive damage multiplier on all targets.
Ascendancy 4
Focal Point
Projectile ConvergenceArrows converge on targetSingle-target DPS boost
Forces multiple projectiles to converge on the target you aim at. Massive single-target damage boost — especially powerful with Tornado Shot on bosses.

Skills · Gem Setup
⚡
Lightning Arrow (Primary)
Main clear skill. Each hit creates chaining beams that arc to 3 nearby enemies. Freeze + Shock on every hit. Supported by: Fork, Overpower, Lightning Penetration, Added Lightning Damage.
🔱
Lightning Rod
Boss skill. Plant 2–3 Rods at the boss's feet, then fire Lightning Arrow at the Rods. Creates devastating burst damage in a fixed area. Supported by: Concentrated Effect, Lightning Penetration, Increased Critical Damage.
🌪️
Tornado Shot
Single-target finisher. Fires a tornado that seeks out the target. Use with Voltaic Mark for maximum single-target DPS. Supported by: Added Lightning Damage, Overpower, Lightning Penetration.
⛈️
Herald of Thunder
Passive aura. When you kill a Shocked enemy with Lightning Arrow, Herald of Thunder triggers a massive cold/lightning explosion. This is what makes map clear godlike. Always active.
🛡️
Lightning Aegis
Defensive aura. Provides flat damage reduction that triggers on Crit. Since Deadeye crits constantly = near-permanent 20% less damage taken from Shocked enemies. Replaces Haste for Spirit efficiency.
👻
Ghost Dance
Defensive skill. Uses Evasion Rating to restore Energy Shield on being Hit. Essential for surviving burst damage. Keep maxed for the tanky "glass cannon that isn't really glass" Deadeye feel.
🎯
Voltaic Mark
Boss curse. Applies a mark that increases Lightning damage taken. Use before Tornado Shot/Lightning Rod rotation on bosses. Significant single-target DPS increase.
❄️
Freezing Salvo
Secondary clear / CC. Fires a salvo of freezing arrows. Use on large packs or tanky rares to ensure Freeze buildup before Lightning Arrow chains. Also useful for Freeze-dependent Herald of Thunder procs.
⚠️ 0.5.0 Change: Lightning Arrow Beams can no longer chain to the same target. This slightly reduces single-target DPS from beams but doesn't affect pack clear — beams will simply arc to different targets in a pack instead.

Rotation · Playstyle

🗺 MAP CLEAR

1
Move aggressively forward
Deadeye clears THROUGH packs — not around them. Move into range, shoot, let lightning chains do the work. Gathering Winds stacks Action Speed with every kill.
2
Lightning Arrow spam
Fire continuously into packs. Each shot chains to 3 enemies — at full Tailwind stacks you fire fast enough to clear full screens before enemies reach you.
3
Herald of Thunder chain
When shocked enemies die, Herald triggers. Let the explosion chain fire automatically — it will clear what Lightning Arrow started. You just keep moving forward.
4
Freezing Salvo on tanky rares
For rare monsters that survive the initial chain — Freezing Salvo to freeze them, then Lightning Arrow to detonate. Or just Lightning Rod placement and fire.

⚔ BOSS ROTATION

1
Voltaic Mark
Apply Voltaic Mark curse immediately — increases Lightning damage taken. Always first action on any boss.
2
Plant 2–3 Lightning Rods
Plant Rods at or near the boss position. Stationary bosses take the full burst — mobile bosses require Rods placed in their path. This is your primary burst tool.
3
Lightning Arrow → Rods
Fire Lightning Arrow at the Rods to detonate them — devastating burst. Reapply Rods as they expire (every ~4 sec). This is the core boss loop.
4
Tornado Shot (off cooldown)
Use between Rod detonations for additional single-target damage. Tornado Shot + Focal Point Ascendancy = significant boss DPS addition.
5
Keep moving
Never stand still. Use Ghost Dance if hit. Kite boss attacks between your own attack windows. Deadeye defense is mobility — not tankiness.

Patch Notes · 0.5.0 Impact
✅ Patch 0.5.0 — Return of the Ancients: Lightning Arrow Deadeye remains S-Tier in 0.5.0. One minor nerf, otherwise unchanged. Bullseye Ascendancy was buffed in 0.4 and carries forward.
🟡 MINOR NERF · Lightning Arrow
Beams can no longer chain to the same target
Lightning beams now always arc to different targets. Slight reduction in single-target DPS from beams — but in practice irrelevant for pack clear since there are always multiple targets. Against single bosses, Lightning Rod is your primary damage source anyway.
🟢 CARRY-FORWARD BUFF · Bullseye
Still grants 10 Critical Weakness stacks (buffed in 0.4)
Bullseye was doubled from 5 to 10 stacks in 0.4. This carries forward into 0.5.0 — permanent massive Crit multiplier on all targets via constant Deadeye crits.
🟢 NEW CONTENT · Runes of Aldur Crafting
Bow crafting significantly improved via Verisium Runeforging
The new Runes of Aldur league introduces the Verisium Runeforging system which can add Runic Ward to armours and upgrade gear via Ancient Runes. Weapon-type Ancient Runes (for Bows) can significantly boost your bow stats without relying on trade.

Pro Tips
Mana is your #1 early problem: Until you have a Sapphire Jewel with 2% Mana on Kill, you will run out of Mana constantly. Farm one ASAP or trade for it. A second source on the Quiver solves Mana permanently.
Keep moving always: Deadeye defense is mobility. Ghost Dance restores ES when hit — but the best defense is never getting hit. Move after every Lightning Arrow shot. Never let a boss attack animation complete while you're stationary.
Lightning Rod placement: Plant Rods BEFORE the boss phase starts. On mobile bosses plant them in doorways or chokepoints. The Rod detonation radius is generous — you don't need perfect placement, just roughly near the target.
Resistance caps first: 75% all resistances before any damage investment. Deadeye is a glass cannon — if you're not Dodge/Evading a hit, you're taking full damage. Resistance caps cut the damage you do take in half.
Freeze before Herald: Herald of Thunder only triggers when you kill a SHOCKED enemy. Lightning Arrow automatically Shocks on hit — just make sure enemies are Shocked (yellow outline) before your kill shot. Freezing Salvo can help freeze tanky targets first.
Best Content: All Maps (fastest clear in PoE2), Pinnacle Bosses (Lightning Rod burst), Breach content, Delirium (speed clears fog before it resets). Self-found viable to red maps — endgame requires trade for a high pDPS bow.
