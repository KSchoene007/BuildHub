Build Overview

Spirit Army Witch is the premier Infernalist Minion build for PoE2 0.5 Return of the Ancients — ranked #2 minion build overall. Command a full specialized undead army: Power Zealot Spectres as the main DPS carry, Raging Spirits via Volcano for AoE, Skeletal Warriors with Amanamu's Tithe on the frontline, Storm Mages for Shock, and the Infernal Hound for the Loyalty damage reduction buff. Entirely unaffected by the Infernal Legion nerf.
⚠️ Act 3 Milestone: The build fully comes online when you raise Power Zealot Spectres in the Crematorium. Before Act 3: Skeletal Warriors + Raging Spirits carry you through the campaign.

Ascendancy — Infernalist

✅ Infernalist is chosen for Pyromantic Pact (infinite resource sustain) and Loyalty from Infernal Hound (10% permanent damage reduction). Do NOT invest in Infernal Legion nodes.
① First — Loyalty
Infernal Hound
Infernal Hound reduces damage you take by 10% while near you (Loyalty buff). Essential passive survivability for the entire game. Always keep the Hound alive.
② Priority — Pyromantic Pact
Infinite Resource Sustain
Removes mana — replaces with Infernal Flame. When full, damages you (inverted with Recoup tech). Enables infinite casting with no resource management.
③ Damage — Hellbound
Minion Damage Multiplier
Minions deal more damage when near you. Stay close to your army during combat. Pairs with Hellbent Commander passive for stacked multipliers.
④ Defense — Soul Eater
Kill Stacks → Bonus Stats
Gain stacks from minion kills — bonus damage and movement speed. Excellent during map clear, reaches high stack counts quickly with a full army.
⚠️ AVOID: Do NOT take any Infernal Legion ascendancy nodes. The gem was nerfed to 10% max Life damage/sec in 0.5 (was 20%). This build does not use Infernal Legion at all.

Leveling Guide · Acts 1–3

💡 Goal: build your minion army as quickly as possible. By Act 2 you should be hands-off — your skeletons clear everything while you manage curses.
Act 1
Start with Bone Spear
Damage + corpse generation
Use Bone Spear to kill enemies and generate corpses. Unlock Raise Skeleton as soon as available and start building your army. Focus weapon upgrades over everything else.
💡 First priority: find a sceptre with +1 to Minion Skill Gems. Even a low-level version more than doubles early army damage.
Act 1–2
Unlock Raise Skeleton + Skeletal Mages
Army foundation
Max Raise Skeleton and Skeletal Mage ranks. Each rank increases damage and army size. By level 15 you should have a full frontline and supporting mages. Unlock Infernal Hound when available — it immediately provides the Loyalty damage reduction buff.
💡 Infernal Hound: use its active ability on Elite monsters to taunt them. Keeps dangerous enemies from reaching you while your army destroys them.
Act 2
Unlock Pain Offering
Core buff skill online
Pain Offering becomes available. Add it to your rotation immediately: Volcano (later) or Bone Spear → Pain Offering. The +40% attack speed buff to all minions is enormous. This is when the build starts to feel very strong.
💡 Rotation from here: spawn minions → cast Pain Offering → let army clear. Refresh Pain Offering every 8 seconds.
Act 3
Find Power Zealot Spectres
Main carry unlocked
Critical milestone. Power Zealot Spectres are found in the Act 3 Crematorium. When you see their corpses, cast Raise Spectre immediately. These become your primary DPS for the rest of the game — they deal dramatically more damage than any skeleton variant.
💡 You can only have 1–2 Spectres early without Spirit investment. Prioritize Spirit on gear to unlock a third Spectre slot ASAP.
Act 3
Replace Flame Wall with Volcano
0.5 update
Once Volcano is available, replace Flame Wall in your links. Volcano summons Raging Spirits with automatic ignite application — much cleaner than Flame Wall. Your full 0.5 rotation is now live: Volcano → Pain Offering → Elemental Weakness → move.
Endgame
Transition: Uul-Netol's Embrace
Spectre DPS leap
Once you reach maps, switch your Raise Spectre support from Minion Mastery to Uul-Netol's Embrace. This unique support gem massively boosts Spectre DPS and is the single biggest endgame upgrade for this build.
💡 Skill bar finalized: Volcano · Raise Spectre · Skeletal Warriors · Pain Offering · Elemental Weakness · Frost Bomb

Passive Tree Priority

ℹ Witch starts top-left (Intelligence section). Path toward Minion Damage clusters first, then survivability nodes.
① Minion Damage Notables
Highest priority from level 1. Every Minion Damage notable near the Witch start multiplies the damage of all 10+ minions simultaneously. Take all reachable notables before branching out.
② Hellbent Commander ×3
Large [x] multiplier to all minion damage while near your army. Must-have. Max all 3 points as soon as available. Always stay near your minions during combat to maintain the buff.
③ Inspiring Leader ×3
Minion Attack Speed — increases how fast Skeletal Warriors, Mages, and Spectres attack. Directly multiplies DPS of the entire army.
④ Minion Life Nodes
Prevents your army from dying — especially important for Spectres who cannot auto-resummon. Path through Life nodes near minion clusters.
⑤ Spirit Investment
Spirit allows more minions to be active simultaneously. Path toward Spirit nodes to unlock a 3rd Spectre slot and more Skeletal Warriors/Mages.
⑥ Death's Embrace ×3
Shadow damage bonus when near enemies. Synergizes with staying close to your army (Hellbent Commander area). Take after core minion nodes.
⑦ Energy Shield / Runic Ward
Defensive layer. In 0.5 ES was nerfed — prioritize nodes that grant Runic Ward or flat ES rather than % ES increase. Combined with Verisium Runeforging on gear.
⑧ Resistance Pathing
Cap all resistances at 75% as you level. Path through resistance nodes on the way to damage clusters whenever possible.
⚠ DO NOT invest in Fire Damage, Infernal Legion, or Burning passives. This build deals Physical/Chaos damage through minions, not fire DoT.

Rotation & Playstyle

🗺 MAP CLEAR
1. Cast Volcano — Raging Spirits spawn and ignite enemies
2. Cast Pain Offering immediately after — +40% army attack speed
3. Cast Elemental Weakness on packs — -20% resistances
4. Move forward — Spectres and Warriors clear everything passively
5. Refresh Volcano every ~8s to maintain Raging Spirit count
⚔ BOSS ROTATION
1. Frost Bomb on boss — cold exposure + -90% life regen
2. Elemental Weakness — resistance shred
3. Volcano to refresh Raging Spirits
4. Pain Offering immediately after
5. Command Warriors onto boss — focus-attack burst
6. Refresh Volcano + Pain Offering every 7–8s
✅ Key Insight: Power Zealot Spectres handle most boss DPS passively. Your job is buff maintenance — Volcano → Pain Offering every ~8 seconds. Simple 2-button rhythm once mastered.
⚠️ Stay Near Your Army: Hellbound (ascendancy) and Hellbent Commander (passive) both require proximity to your minions. Never kite far from your Spectres and Warriors — you lose significant DPS multipliers.

Pro Tips

Power Zealot Spectres First
The moment you reach Act 3 Crematorium, use Raise Spectre on every Power Zealot corpse you see. They are your main DPS for the rest of the game. If you die and lose them, return to Act 3 to re-raise.
Volcano → Pain Offering Rhythm
Your core rotation is a 2-button rhythm: Volcano (spawn Raging Spirits) → Pain Offering (buff army). Repeat every 7–8 seconds. Missing Pain Offering is a 40% DPS loss.
Stay Near Your Army
Two passive multipliers require proximity: Hellbent Commander and Hellbound (ascendancy). Never kite more than 20 units from your Spectres or you lose significant [x] damage multipliers.
Runeforging Week 1 Priority
When you find Enfolding Dawn (100 Spirit unique), do NOT equip it. Destroy it via Verisium Runeforging to create a Spirit rune. Socket into your sceptre or helm for a permanent Spirit boost without wearing the item.
Shock Uptime = +20% DPS
Skeletal Storm Mages apply Shock, causing enemies to take 20% more damage from all sources. Summon at least 2–3 Storm Mages and keep them alive. This passive buff affects your Spectres, Warriors, and Raging Spirits simultaneously.
Boss: Pre-Stack before Room
Before a boss room, cast Volcano twice and Pain Offering to have full Raging Spirit count and buffed army. Enter the boss fight at full strength — never re-setup mid-fight.
Uul-Netol's Embrace is the Endgame Leap
When transitioning to maps, replace Minion Mastery with Uul-Netol's Embrace in your Raise Spectre links. This single gem swap is worth more DPS than 10 item upgrades combined.
Infernal Hound Active Ability
The Hound's active skill taunts all nearby enemies and makes it Unstoppable. Use this proactively when dangerous Elites spawn near you — it buys your army 3–4 seconds of uninterrupted DPS.
