Build Overview

The Heartbreaker build combines massive DPS with exceptional survivability through the Heartstopper talent: headshots mark enemies, and every hit on marked enemies grants +1% Bonus Armor & +1% Weapon Damage (up to 50–100 stacks).

Core Synergy: Kingbreaker AR (Perfect Flatline) + Technician (Linked Laser Pointer) → every hit automatically marks enemies → Heartstopper stacks build without needing headshots → maximum Damage & Armor simultaneously.

⚠ 1-Exotic Rule: In The Division 2 you can only equip ONE exotic armor piece at a time. Choose your exotic combination accordingly.

Pro Tips

🔫 Farming Kingbreaker
Very low drop rate. Best sources: Countdown and Summit Heroic with AR Targeted Loot. Named AR also drops from missions and Exotic Caches. Farm consistently — it's worth it.
🎯 Linked Laser Pointer on BOTH Weapons
Without this mod on both weapons you must land headshots to mark. With it: every hit marks automatically → stacks build effortlessly at all times. Never equip without it.
🔄 ACS-12 as Stack Reset Tool
After any combat pause your stacks decay fast. A few shotgun blasts with the ACS-12 instantly rebuilds them — then swap back to Kingbreaker for full damage output.
🛡️ Chest Choice: Max BPM vs The Closer
Solo / Legendary → Max BPM (100 stacks = more Armor). Groups / Raids → The Closer (Spotter +20% raw damage). Both are excellent — match to your content.
🏆 Best Content
Heroic & Legendary Missions, The Summit, Countdown, Iron Horse Raid (DPS role). Scales excellently at all difficulty levels — one of the most versatile builds in the entire game.
