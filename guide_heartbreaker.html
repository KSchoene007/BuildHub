Build Overview

The Heartbreaker build combines massive DPS with exceptional survivability through the Heartstopper talent: headshots mark enemies, and every subsequent hit on marked enemies grants +1% Bonus Armor and +1% Weapon Damage (up to 50–100 stacks).

Core Synergy: Kingbreaker AR (Perfect Flatline) + Technician (Linked Laser Pointer) → every hit automatically marks enemies → Heartstopper stacks build without needing headshots → maximum Damage & Armor simultaneously.

Exotic Combinations
Option A – Memento Backpack (Recommended for Survivability):
Memento as only Exotic → Picaro's Holster (Named, +15% WD) or Heartbreaker Holster. Chest = Max BPM Heartbreaker (100 stacks). Backpack = Memento with Kill Confirmed (+30% WD + Armor Regen). Best choice for Solo PvE and Legendary content.
Option B – Coyote's Mask (Crit Focus, Best for Max DPS):
Coyote's Mask as only Exotic → Heartbreaker Backpack (Cold talent, +2% Armor/stack). Picaro's Holster (Named) for +15% WD. Chest = The Closer (Named, Spotter +20%). For players who prefer maximum Crit Damage output.
Option C – No Exotic (Full Set Control):
6× Heartbreaker — all set bonuses active, Chest (Max BPM) and Backpack (Cold). Picaro's Holster (Named) for +15% WD. No exotic dependency, easier to farm. Solid choice for players new to the build.

Pro Tips
Farming Kingbreaker: Named AR drops in missions / Summit floors with AR Targeted Loot. Very low drop rate — Countdown and Summit Heroic with AR loot are the best sources. Exotic Caches can also contain it.
Linked Laser Pointer on BOTH weapons: Without this mod you must land headshots to mark enemies. With the mod every hit marks automatically → stacks build effortlessly at all times.
ACS-12 as Stack Reset: After a combat pause stacks are gone. A few shotgun blasts instantly rebuild them — then swap back to Kingbreaker for full damage.
Chest Choice: Max BPM (Heartbreaker Chest, 100 stacks) or The Closer (Named, Spotter +20%)? For Solo and Legendary → Max BPM for more Armor. For Groups / Raids → The Closer for more raw damage.
Play Aggressively: This build requires active shooting to maintain stacks. The Crusader Shield and Memento Armor Regen make this safely possible — stay on the offensive.
Best Content: Heroic & Legendary Missions, The Summit, Countdown, Iron Horse Raid (DPS role). Scales excellently at all difficulty levels — one of the most versatile builds in the entire game.
