Build Overview

The Chameleon Exotic Assault Rifle rewards hitting all body areas (head, body, legs). The more varied your hits, the stronger it becomes. Combined with Striker's Battlegear for massive DPS stacking.

Playstyle: Close-range SMG-like (up to 1035 RPM), very fast Striker bonus stacking. Keep all three Adaptive Instinct buffs active simultaneously for maximum output — they all stack and last 45 seconds.

Pro Tips

🦎 Keep All 3 Buffs Active
At 1035 RPM all three Adaptive Instinct buffs activate within the first seconds of combat. Maintain them by never stopping fire — 45 second duration is generous.
🔫 SMG Secondary is Mandatory
The Chameleon has lower baseline Crit Chance. The SMG's 21% Crit Damage as secondary attribute is the only way to compensate — no other weapon class provides this.
🏆 Memento Kill Farming
Clear trash before boss rooms to reach 30 kills for maximum Kill Confirmed duration. Enter every boss with +30% WD + Armor Regen active.
🎯 Best Content
Heroic & Legendary Missions, Summit, Countdown. Chameleon is especially strong in dense enemy encounters where all three buffs maintain themselves naturally.
