Build Overview

The Chameleon build uses the Exotic Chameleon Assault Rifle combined with the Striker's Battlegear gear set. The Chameleon rewards agents who hit all body areas (head, body, legs) — the more varied your hits, the stronger the weapon becomes.

Playstyle: Close-range oriented (similar to an SMG), very high RPM (up to 1035), fast Striker bonus stacking. Keep all three Adaptive Instinct buffs active simultaneously for maximum output.

Pro Tips
Activate all 3 buffs: When firing at normal enemies, head, body and legs are automatically hit. After roughly one magazine all three Adaptive Instinct buffs are active simultaneously — that's the build's "sweet spot".
Hold Striker Stacks: At 1035 RPM the 50 Striker's Gamble stacks build very fast. Don't stop shooting — step out of cover briefly, eliminate enemies quickly.
Collect Memento Trophies: After every kill pick up the trophies! The long-term buff (30 kills) from Kill Confirmed gives +30% Weapon Damage for 5 minutes — don't miss it.
Close-range Playstyle: Despite being classified as an AR, the Chameleon has more of an SMG engagement range. Always push close to enemies, use cover, shoot fast.
Best Content: Control Points (Heroic), The Summit, Heroic Missions, Countdown. For Legendary content consider a more defensive variant with more blue cores.
