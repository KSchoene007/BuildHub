Shadow Warrior

Minion Necromancer · Season 13

Diablo 4 · Lord of Hatred · Season of Reckoning · S-Tier
Necromancer S-Tier Speed Farm Pit Push All Content
🏆 Season 13 Meta: Minion Necromancer reached S-Tier after the Lord of Hatred rework. All minions converted to Shadow (Darkness) damage — stack +Darkness Skills on gear to buff ALL minions simultaneously. Bloodless Scream provides a ×3.5 damage multiplier and perma-freezes everything on screen.
💀
Build Overview
✓ Strengths
✓Up to 28 active minions simultaneously
✓Passive playstyle — minions do all the work
✓Perma-freeze via Bloodless Scream
✓Excellent in both Speed Farm and Pit Push
✓Army of the Dead = massive burst every ~10 sec
✓Very tanky — minions absorb all damage
✗ Weaknesses
✗Bloodless Scream required for full power
✗3 of 6 skill slots used by minion skills
✗Talisman system requires 6-slot Seal
✗Blood Mist required for Overpower stacking

Core Mechanic: Convert all minions to Shadow damage → they become Darkness skills → stack +Darkness Skills on all gear pieces. Bloodless Scream applies Chill scaling up to 250% on frozen targets, providing a ×3.5 damage multiplier. Army of the Dead on a ~10-second cooldown nukes elite packs for 1350%+ damage.

Season 13 Changes: Minion skills moved into the Skill Tree (not Book of the Dead). Skeletal Warriors now have an active Command ability. New Uniques Deathgrip (+6 Warriors) and The Undercrown (+6 Mages) allow up to 28 minions simultaneously. New Talisman system converts any Unique into a Charm — wear gloves + helmet AND benefit from both as Talismans.

Skill Setup
⚠️ Important: With Season 13 all minion skills are in the Skill Tree. You use 3 of 6 skill slots for minions (Warriors, Mages, Golem). The remaining 3 slots are utility/survival.
Skeletal Warriors Reaper · CommandActive + Passive
Primary damage dealers. Choose Reaper variant (+50% damage). Use Command ability to focus-fire elites and bosses — increases their damage taken by 30–40%. This is your main burst tool for single targets.
Skeletal Mages Shadow · BarrierActive + Passive
Shadow variant for the Barrier node. Make mages Shadow Darkness skills: click Bone Mages → back to Shadow → summon. This is important for the Darkness stacking synergy. Barrier provides passive damage reduction.
Golem Iron · Gargantua AuraActive + Passive
Iron Golem with Gargantua upgrade — increases size and provides an aura that boosts attack speed and movement speed of all other minions. Sacrifice node for attack speed bonus while keeping the Golem.
Army of the Dead Prime · SupremeUltimate · 5 points
Your nuke. With Unyielding Commander aspect: while active minions deal +50% summon damage + 2% per minion owned. At 25 minions = +100% bonus. Total damage floor: 1350%+ per cast. Use on every Elite pack and Boss.
Sever MobilityMovement
Primary movement skill — faster than Sorcerer Teleport in endgame. Creates corpses on cast which your Warriors immediately consume. Essential for map traversal.
Blood Mist Survival · OverpowerUtility
Get-out-of-jail button AND your primary way to maintain 4 stacks of Overpower for the Dominate Glyph. Use when taking heavy damage or when manually stacking Overpower before boss encounters.
💡 Cursed Aura Aspect: Once you have the Aspect of the Cursed Aura, remove Iron Maiden and Decrepify from your skill bar — the Aura applies them automatically. Frees 2 slots for Blood Mist and Blight (Whirlpool for grouping).

Talisman · Charm System
💡 Season 13 Talisman System: Any Unique item can be converted to a Talisman/Charm. This lets you equip Deathgrip as Gloves AND benefit from a second Unique via a Charm slot. Use a Legendary Seal with +1 Charm Slot to unlock 6 Charm slots total.
Talisman Priority 1
Azurewrath Charm
For Speed Farming / Map Clear: massive AoE clear potential. Convert to Charm and use Bloodless Scream as main weapon. Swap for Red Blessing on difficult Pit content.
Talisman Priority 2
Bloodless Scream Charm
Alternative Charm slot — if you have a second Bloodless Scream convert one to Charm for double Darkness freeze scaling. Very powerful for Pit pushing.
Talisman Priority 3
Black Shroud Charm Set
Full Black Shroud Charm Set (5-piece) takes priority over individual Charms once available. Complete the set before optimizing individual Charm slots.
Talisman Tip
+3 Minion or +3 Darkness Skills
All Charm affixes should target +3 Minion Skills OR +3 Darkness Skills — they are functionally equivalent for this build and both multiply all minion damage.

Rotation & Playstyle

🗺 SPEED FARMING (Helltides / Whispers)

Use Sever to dash through the map. Spam Decrepify on every pack (until you have Cursed Aura Aspect — then it's automatic). Warriors auto-raise from corpses every 0.5 sec. Army of the Dead on every Elite pack. With Azurewrath Charm: everything dies before you even stop moving.

⚔ PIT PUSHING

1
Blood Mist
Use Blood Mist to stack 4× Overpower for Dominate Glyph — essential for maximum damage on the next burst window.
2
Blight (Whirlpool)
Cast Blight on the enemy group to pull them together. Warriors' Deathgrip cleave hits all grouped enemies simultaneously.
3
Warrior Command
Use Warrior Command on the main target — increases damage taken by 30–40% for focused burst.
4
Army of the Dead
Activate Army of the Dead — 1350%+ damage nuke with Unyielding Commander + minion count multiplier. With ~10 sec CDR this is nearly permanent.
5
Repeat
Minions auto-attack constantly. Your only job is Blood Mist for Overpower stacks and Army of the Dead on cooldown.

Pro Tips
Darkness Conversion is mandatory: All minions MUST be Shadow damage to benefit from +Darkness Skills gear stacking. Verify: click Bone Mages → back to Shadow → re-summon. If your mages are not Shadow they do not count as Darkness skills.
Talisman System: Get a Legendary Seal with +1 Charm Slot ASAP to unlock 6 Charm slots. Use Azurewrath for clear, swap to Red Blessing for Pit boss phases. You can swap Charms mid-Pit!
Attack Speed cap first: 100% Attack Speed is your absolute first priority — minions inherit it directly. Until you hit 100% AS, ignore damage multipliers entirely. Same applies to 100% Crit Chance.
Army of the Dead CDR: Aim for ~7 second cooldown reduction on Army of the Dead. With corpse generation from Warriors (every 0.5 sec) and Sever casting, you should have permanent uptime. This is your single biggest DPS lever.
Without Bloodless Scream: The build is fully viable for all content up to mid-tier Pit without it. Focus on +Darkness Skills on every gear piece and use Azurewrath as main weapon until Bloodless drops.
Best Content: Helltides, Whispers (Speed Farm) · Infernal Hordes · Pit Tier 60–100+ (with full gear) · Bossing (Warrior Command + Army of the Dead burst). One of the most versatile builds in Season 13.
