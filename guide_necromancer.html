01

Build Overview

Shadow Warrior runs up to 28 minions simultaneously — Deathgrip (+6 Warriors) + Undercrown (+6 Mages). Convert ALL minions to Shadow damage → they become Darkness skills → stack +Darkness Skills on all gear.

Bloodless Scream applies Chill scaling up to 250% on frozen targets = ×3.5 damage multiplier. Army of the Dead on ~10 sec cooldown nukes elite packs for 1350%+ damage. Season 13 Changes: Skeletal Warriors now have Command ability. New Talisman system converts Uniques to Charms.

✓ Strengths
Up to 28 active minions simultaneously
Perma-freeze via Bloodless Scream
Army of the Dead = massive burst every ~10 sec
Very tanky — minions absorb all damage
Excellent in Speed Farm and Pit Push
✗ Weaknesses
Bloodless Scream required for full power
3 of 6 skill slots used by minion skills
Talisman system requires 6-slot Seal
Blood Mist required for Overpower stacking

Primary movement skill. Creates corpses on cast which Warriors consume. Faster than Teleport in endgame.
🌫

Get-out-of-jail AND primary way to maintain 4 stacks of Overpower for Dominate Glyph.

Talisman / Charm System

💡 Season 13 Talisman System: Any Unique can be converted to a Charm. Equip Deathgrip as Gloves AND benefit from a 2nd Unique via Charm slot. Use a Legendary Seal with +1 Charm Slot for 6 total slots.
🗡
Azurewrath Charm — Speed Farm
For Speed Farming / Map Clear: massive AoE clear. Convert to Charm + use Bloodless Scream as main weapon. Swap for Red Blessing on difficult Pit.
💀
Bloodless Scream Charm
2nd Bloodless as Charm = double Darkness freeze scaling. Very powerful for Pit pushing.
🛡
Black Shroud Charm Set (Priority)
Full 5-piece Black Shroud Charm Set takes priority over individual Charms once available.
💎
Charm Affixes
Target: +3 Minion Skills OR +3 Darkness Skills on ALL Charm affixes — they are functionally equivalent and both multiply all minion damage.

07

Pro Tips

🔮 Darkness Conversion is Mandatory
All minions MUST be Shadow damage for +Darkness Skills stacking to work. Verify: click Bone Mages → back to Shadow → re-summon. If not Shadow: they don't count.
⚔ Attack Speed Cap First
100% Attack Speed is your absolute first priority — minions inherit it directly. Until you hit 100% AS, ignore all damage multipliers entirely.
⏱ Army of the Dead CDR
Aim for ~7 sec cooldown reduction. With corpse generation from Warriors (every 0.5 sec) and Sever, you should have near-permanent uptime. This is your biggest DPS lever.
🎭 Talisman Swap Mid-Pit
You can swap Charm slots mid-Pit run! Use Azurewrath for trash clear, swap to Red Blessing for the boss phase. No restrictions.
